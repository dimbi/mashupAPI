Realtime live input maps

The international maps are plotted based on real-time user input you jus generated. The more you people use this website, the more accurate and complete the maps and chart is. Morover, maps is also enhanced with animation which will plot links between migration source and destination countries. Mousover on any countries will display the data related to each country.




Migration chord diagram

Mouseover the circle outer ring next to the country of interest to show the to total number of immigrants from that country. In the future this will also have top 5 country of destination and the respective number of Immigrants to those countries. The thickness of links between countries represents encodes the number of people moving between tho countries.The thicker lines means larger number of people. Mousover on the chord (string) connecting two countries will show the number of immigrants of those two countries. Play around by hovering over edges and chords!

Source: Global Migrant Origin Database.
